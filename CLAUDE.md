## WORDS I USE (so you always understand me)
- COAM = Coin Operated Amusement Machine (Georgia gaming category)
- TITO = Ticket In Ticket Out (how players cash out winnings)
- SAS = Slot Accounting System (machine talks to back office)
- RTP = Return To Player (percentage the game pays back over time)
- JAMMA = Japan Amusement Machine and Marketing Association (36-pin wiring standard)
- GLI = Gaming Laboratories International (the certification company)
- Hit frequency = how often a spin wins something (we target 32%)
- Hold = percentage the machine keeps (100% minus RTP)
- Volatility = how spread out wins are (medium = mix of small and big)
- rAF = requestAnimationFrame (the fast animation method)
- Canvas = the drawing area in HTML5 where the game graphics appear
- Kiosk mode = fullscreen browser with no toolbar or buttons showing
- Fire Link = the benchmark slot game by Light and Wonder we model after
- Banilla Fusion = competitor game board series ($6,500 per unit)
